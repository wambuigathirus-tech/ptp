About Me

Samuel Njoroge, professionally known as "Priest the Poet," is a leader in Kenya's creative space. He combines art with business and community leadership, working at the intersection of the Orange Economy - the creative economy that drives innovation and cultural development.

Through his platform, he mentors emerging artists and drives artistic excellence while creating spaces for storytellers. His spoken word performances are both socially conscious and professionally executed, aiming to influence society through the power of words.

As a community builder, Samuel focuses on fostering creative communities and supporting the growth of Kenya's artistic landscape.
